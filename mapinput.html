Instructions:

Begin by initializing the viewport of the map. (This is the first location that will be shown while displaying the map)
Next, choose a value for the zoom(0 being the farthest and 22 being the closest)
Add at least one marker. Enter the coordinate and that is where the marker will be placed.
You can add as many markers as you like by clicking on 'Add Marker' button.
Click Generate to see the map.

Notice that once you add a valid initial viewport's coordinate and a zoom value, you cannot make changes to them. Errors will be displayed on the screen.
